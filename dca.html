Disney's California Adventure

Disney's California Adventure first opened its gates on February 8, 2001. It was constructed on what once was the Disneyland parking lot.

It it home to the breathtaking nighttime extravaganza - World of Color.

Premiering on June 11, 2010 at Paradise Gardens Park, this spectacular show brings animation to life, where you can relive memorable scenes from beloved Disney and Pixar films. Powerful dancing fountains shoot high to the sky on Paradise Bay, creating a fantastic movie screen of water and a veil of mist, while lasers electrify the night, capped with a soaring soundtrack. You may see scenes such as WALL-E and Eve zipping through the cosmos, Pocahontas exploring just around the river bend and Jack Sparrow battling on the sea.
