Clarabelle's Hand Scooped Ice Cream

In the mood for something delicious? Customize a hand-dipped ice cream bar or treat yourself to a sundae, cone, or cup at this Buena Vista Street ice cream parlor.

  • Location: Disneyland's California Adventure • Buena Vista Street
  • Between Trolley Treats & Fiddler, Fifer and Practical Cafe

California Churro Co.

Here you will find Cinnamon Sugar Churros, Salted Caramel Churros, Apple Pie Churros, Strawberry Cheesecake Churros & even assorted Dipped Churros!

  • Location: Downtown Disney • 2 Cart/Kiosk locations
  • World of Disney (nearby) store entrance • & • Disney's Pin Traders (nearby) kiosk

Mickey Pretzel

This unique treat shaped like the iconic Mickey Mouse, can be enjoyed with or without nacho cheese dipping sauce!

  • Location: Disneyland & Disney's California Adventure
  • Coca-Cola Refreshment Corner (Disneyland) & Bayside Brews (Disney's California Adventure)
  • Also available at additional kiosk locations throughout both parks.

Gibson Girl Ice Cream Parlor

At this old-fashioned ice cream parlor you'll find sweetly nostalgic sundaes dressed up with freshly baked waffle cups & cookies. Or keep it classic with floats, hand-scooped cups & cones.

  • Location: Disneyland • Main Street
  • Between Carnation Cafe & Candy Palace

Seasonal Cupcake

Assorted varieties offered throughout the year

  • Location for World of Color: Disneyland's California Adventure
  • at Paradise Gardens Park - located across from The Little Mermaid ride

Disney After Dark Desserts

Disney After Dark is a special type of themed/ticketed event (it does not include general admission entry). Theme night's such as SWEETHEART'S NITE, STAR WARS NITE, PRINCESS NITE & THROWBACK NITE, are among the "Special Events" currently being offered throughout the year.

  • Location: Disneyland
  • Assorted Restaurant & Kiosk locations (refer to special event guide for details)
