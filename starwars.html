Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge

Buckle your seat belts and get ready for the ride of a lifetime. Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge at Disneyland park will transport you to a galaxy far, far away. You can now visit the world of Batuu and Black Spire Outpost, an infamous stop for traders, adventurers, and smugglers traveling around the Outer Rim and Wild Space. While on the planet Batuu, you may spot some iconic starships and vehicles up-close. Whether you're part of the Resistance or supporting the First Order, activate your hyper drive and head on over to Star Wars: Galaxy's Edge!
